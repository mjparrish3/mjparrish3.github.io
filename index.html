Who we are

Section 31 is the "DevOps" branch of Kobayashi Maru whose mission is to continuously deliver impactful software Space Operators love.

Section 31 is initially focused on modernizing SDA capabilities and deprecating current legacy SSA systems, executing exciting projects that are building new capabilities and solving urgent pain points by partnering with Silicon Valley and utilizing commercial software development best practices.

Vision

Create a digital Space Force where extrodinary software propels space dominance.

Mission

Continuously deliver impactful software Space Operators love.

Leadership

Our goal is to inspire people more than manage them. We trust our teams to do what they think is best for Section 31-giving them a high degree of freedom, power, and information in support of their decisions. Small diverse teams drive our work and 90% of the decisions are made by them. In turn, this generates a sense of responsibility and self-discipline that drives us to do great work that benefits the organization.

Maj Matthew Holland

Director, Section 31

Director of Product
